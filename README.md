# 🌾 KisanConnect – Hyperlocal Farmer-Buyer Marketplace

> 🚀 Connecting farmers directly with nearby buyers — no middlemen, better prices, transparent ecosystem.

---

## 📌 Overview

**KisanConnect** is a hyperlocal digital marketplace designed to bridge the gap between farmers and buyers such as restaurants, vendors, and shops.

It enables:
- Farmers to sell directly
- Buyers to purchase fresh produce locally
- Transparent pricing & better profit margins

This project demonstrates a **real-world scalable frontend architecture** with modern UI/UX.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Farmers often rely on intermediaries, reducing their profit margins.  
Buyers struggle to find fresh, affordable produce locally.

👉 **KisanConnect solves this by enabling direct farmer-to-buyer interaction.**

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧠 Smart Features
- 🔹 AI-based price suggestions
- 🔹 Demand indicators (High / Medium / Low)
- 🔹 Distance-based product discovery
- 🔹 Urgent sale & market alerts
- 🔹 Farmer rating & trust system

---

### 👨‍🌾 Farmer Features
- Add & manage product listings
- View offers from buyers
- Dashboard with stats
- Notification system

---

### 🛒 Buyer Features
- Browse nearby products
- Filter by price, distance, demand
- View detailed product info
- Make offers to farmers
- Save & track products

### 📱 Mobile Experience
- Bottom navigation (Home, Search, Alerts, Profile)

- Touch-friendly interactions
